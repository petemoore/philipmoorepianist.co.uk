##### Petrushka

After two lockdown cancellations, Simon and I finally played Victor Babin's highly virtuosic two-piano transcription of 3 movements from Petrushka, in a BBC Radio 3 lunchtime recital from LSO St Lukes in January 2022. Also in the concert was Rachmaninov's two-piano version of his enchanting Symphonic Dances.
